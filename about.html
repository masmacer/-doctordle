About Doctordle

Doctordle is an independent diagnosis guessing game built around short clinical cases, daily puzzle loops, and topic-specific challenge modes.

What the site is

Doctordle turns medical-style case prompts into a game. You read clues one at a time, decide when to guess, and try to land on the diagnosis before the full case is revealed. The site includes a main daily case, an endless mode, and several topic pages such as Emergency Medicine, Dermatology, Pediatrics, Digestive Health, Women's Health, Cardiology, Dental, and Mental Health.

Independence and affiliation

Doctordle.net is an independent project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or associated with doctordle.org. The site is operated separately and uses its own content structure, pages, and gameplay implementation.

Medical position

Doctordle is for entertainment and general educational interest only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you have any health concerns, you should consult a qualified medical professional.
