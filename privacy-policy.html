Privacy Policy

Doctordle uses a small amount of browser storage to keep gameplay functional and remember your progress across sessions.

What we use

Doctordle uses cookies and local storage to save gameplay progress, remember consent choices, and support features such as daily stats, streaks, and endless mode sessions. This data stays in your browser unless you choose to clear it.

How the data is used

The stored data is used to keep the site working as intended, including saving guesses, preventing accidental resets, and preserving game-related preferences. Doctordle does not use gameplay storage as a substitute for medical records or health advice.

Your choices

You can clear cookies or browser storage at any time through your browser settings. Doing so may remove saved progress, streak data, and consent settings for the site.
